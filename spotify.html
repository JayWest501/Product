02 · Product Logic

The reasoning behind the redesign.

What works

Discovery Weekly and Daily Mix create strong habit loops through predictable, low-effort personalization.

→

What breaks

Search blends music, podcasts, and audiobooks when intent is ambiguous, forcing users to compensate.

→

My fix

Persistent content-type filters and lightweight explainability labels reduce cognitive load without overhauling the product.
